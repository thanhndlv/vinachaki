Coffee

Coffee is one of the main produce of Vietnam and especially the central highlands with the export output standing at number 2 in the world after Brazil.

The abundant coffee trees in the central highlands also offer quality soft wood that can be used for white charcoal (Binchotan) and soft wood charcoal production.
