Softwood
Charcoal

Softwood charcoal is made from the wood of industrial, short-term trees such as Coffee, Khaya, Eucalyptus, which are abundant in the central highlands in Vietnam.

A Barbecue favorite

Softwood Charcoal, especially Coffee Charcoal and Khaya Charcoal, are our best selling products.

Undergoing strict and professional production by experienced manufacturers, our Softwood Charcoal holds the best quality you can possibly find in its grades, highly guaranteed by our testing procedure (available for customer's supervision).

Our Softwood Charcoal are frequently ordered by importers from Australia, China and Middle East countries for BBQ usage.
