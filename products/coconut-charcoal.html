Coconut -
a plant of so many
goodness

Coconut is a very popular tree in Vietnam, especially along the coastline.

Every single part of the Coconut tree is usable and offers economic value, including the Coconut shell.

Coconut shell itself has a lot of applications, with one of them is charcoal production. Coconut charcoal can be used for barbeque, smoking shisha or in industrial applications.
